The Standard

01 / Analysis

The longevity of a surface is decided before the first ton of asphalt hits the ground. We begin with a rigorous assessment of sub-grade physics and drainage requirements, ensuring your investment is engineered for the expansive soils of the Colorado landscape.

02 / Discipline

Our work is quiet, precise, and uncompromising. We choose materials, thicknesses, and sequencing based on how they will live over decades of heavy use and mountain weather—not just how they look on the day of the pour.

03 / Threshold

This is a threshold, not a catalog. We invite you to explore the architectural record of our word. When you are ready for a surface built with intention, we are ready to build it.
