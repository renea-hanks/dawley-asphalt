A Legacy of Business Balance

"There is hardly anything in the world that some man cannot make a little worse and sell a little cheaper..."

Dawley Asphalt began its local operations in Colorado on June 1, 1994. For 31 years, this family-owned enterprise has operated on a singular principle: the neighborly commitment to doing it right the first time.

In the high-altitude landscape of Colorado Springs, asphalt is more than a surface—it is a capital asset. Jerry Dawley has spent three decades refining the craft of paving into a discipline of precision engineering, ensuring every project adheres to the John Ruskin standard of quality and durability.
